World Population Report from 1970 to 2020

This World Population Report presents population data for each decade from 1970 to 2020 and is intended to help provide more visual information for those interested in population change.

The data comes from Kaggle's "World Population Dataset"(https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/iamsouravbanerjee/world-population-dataset), which is recognized as gold and therefore has a high degree of credibility.
